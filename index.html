v to switch camera
under third person camera:
  • mouse wheel to scale
  • drag with LEFT mouse button down to see around
  • W, S to walk
  • Shift to bow
under first person camera:
  • mouse dragging to see around
under free camera:
  • W, S, A, D to fly freely
  • Shift to go up
  • Ctrl to go down
drag with RIGHT mouse button down to rotate the gyro
